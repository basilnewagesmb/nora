A few words about you

To someone who made
every room brighter

There are people who walk into your life and quietly become part of the furniture — the good kind, the kind you never want to imagine the room without. Nora is exactly that. From the very first moment, she brought warmth, laughter, and an endless supply of good ideas to everything she touched.

Whether it was the late-night study sessions that somehow turned into impromptu road trips, or the way she always knew exactly what to say when things got hard — she has a gift for making people feel seen. Watching her take this next leap feels both terrifying and deeply right. The world out there doesn't know what's coming for it.

We are so proud of you. And we will miss you every single day — but we'll smile knowing you're out there making new memories, new friends, and probably a whole new group of people who are lucky to know you.
